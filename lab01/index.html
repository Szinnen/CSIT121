Pork Belly Slices

Pretty easy to cook

  • pork belly slices or strips
  • spices Ex: salt, pepper
  • Flavors Ex: onion powder or garlic powder
  1. Season the meat with your preferred spices or flavor
  2. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees Celsius/ 400 degrees Fahrenheit
  3. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil
  4. Dry the pork slices with a paper towel , and season them both sides
  5. Arrange the strips on the tray and bake until crispy and golden brown, flipping halfway through
  6. Roasting will take between 30 and 40 minutes, depending on the thickness of the meat and your oven

One of my easier and favorite recipes due to how much you can create it with your preference like maiking it healthy by not putting to much salt to just dump as much spices as you can
